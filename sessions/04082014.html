Design Review

Concept, User Personas & User Stories

Present your project's general concept, identifying your user types and what you want them to be able to accomplish through your project.

Type

What font(s) did you choose and how do you use them to help convey your project's message?

Composition

How did you choose to organize your content? Is your layout targeting a particular device form like mobile, tablet or desktop computer?

Design Principles

What design principles are you leveraging to help communicate your project's message?

  • Dominance
  • Similarity
  • Rhythm
  • Texture
  • Direction
  • Contrast

Color

What color scheme did you choose for your project and why?

Javascript

How are you using Javascript in your project and how does this help communicate your message?

User Testing

Divide up into groups, taking turns reviewing each member of your group's Vision Document and comparing it to current state of their project.

  • What is the overall concept for the project
  • What user types have been identified by the User Personas?
  • What features have been identified to meet the needs of the different user types?
  • Which features have been implemented?
  • What do you like about how these features have been implemented?
  • Are there any alternative approaches that might be useful to explore?
  • Are there any features that have not yet been implemented? Identify them.
